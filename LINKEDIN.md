## Video Upload Instructions

1. Open `demo.html` in your browser
2. Press `Win+G` to open Xbox Game Bar → click record
3. Click "Start Auto-Scroll Demo" in the page
4. Let it scroll through all sections (about 40-50 seconds)
5. Stop recording
6. Trim to 30-60 seconds using Clipchamp or any editor
7. Upload as a native video on LinkedIn (not a link — native video gets 5x more views)

## Posting Tips

- Post between 8-10 AM on Tuesday, Wednesday, or Thursday for max reach
- Reply to every comment within the first hour to boost engagement
- Tag 2-3 people in the comments (not the post) who might find it useful
- Add a first comment with your GitHub link to avoid link suppression
